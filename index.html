WHY EATAWAY?

EATAWAY is a culinary website featuring a random recipe generator and a meal planner for your dietary needs! Maybe you don't know what to cook for your next meal? Simply input an ingredient into our recipe generator and it will generate a recipe for you. Trying to create a healthy meal plan? Our meal planner will take into consideration your calorie intake, dietry type and any allergies/food exclusions and help you plan your weekly meal plan. Join our community and let's get cooking together!
